Smart Shopper
Online shopping is full of uncertainty. A user can find a product quickly, but it is much harder to know whether the price is competitive, whether the reviews are trustworthy, whether the item is actually a good fit for their needs, and whether a better option exists somewhere else. Most shoppers end up opening multiple tabs, scanning reviews, checking Reddit, comparing specs, and still making a guess.

This project is about building a shopping companion that reduces that friction. The core idea is a browser extension that detects when a user is viewing a product and surfaces useful decision-making context in the moment. You’ll decide what to include but it could provide price comparisons, review summaries, product pros and cons, alternative recommendations, or signals about whether this is actually a strong choice in its category. And yes even coupons :)

The broader opportunity is not just helping someone spend less, but helping them shop with more confidence. The best version of this product should feel like an informed assistant sitting beside the user while they browse, not just a coupon or cashback layer.
Start Here
Build a browser extension that detects when a user is viewing a product page and surfaces a simple shopping panel. Focus on one or two core features first, such as price comparison across stores or AI-generated review analysis. Get the page detection, product extraction, and recommendation flow working end to end before expanding scope.
Go Further
Consider how the extension can answer higher-level shopping questions, not just show raw data. For example, it could estimate whether a price is a good deal, flag low-quality reviews, summarize who a product is best for, or suggest better alternatives based on what the user cares about (price, quality, shipping, reviews, etc.). You could also tailor the approach by category, since electronics, apparel, and other products may need different logic or data sources. Focus on making the guidance genuinely useful, not just more information.
Suggested Outcomes
A working browser extension that detects at least one type of product page and surfaces useful shopping guidance.
A clear demo of the extraction-to-insight flow, even if the product coverage or recommendation set is limited.
A thoughtful point of view on what kinds of shopping decisions this product helps with best, and where the strongest long-term value sits.
